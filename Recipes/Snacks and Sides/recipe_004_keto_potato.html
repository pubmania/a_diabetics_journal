    Keto Potatoes

    This is really radishes done in a certain way as shown by Joe Duff, but honestly they do taste like potatoes and are so delicious as a side portion in dinner / salads or just as snacks on their own. YouTube Link

    Key Stats


    16.15 Net Carbs per Serving

    Cooking Time: 30 minutes

    Serves: 1 portion

    Type: Vegetarian

    Category: Italian

    • Ingredients

      S.No Ingredient Amount Step
      1. Radishes 500 gms Step 1
      2. Rock Salt 1/2 Tablespoon Step 2
      3. Black Pepper 1/2 Tablespoon Step 2
      4. Italian Herbs 1/2 Tablespoon (optional) Step 3
      5. Olive Oil 2 Tablespoons Step 4

    • Cookware


      1. Bowl
      2. Air Fryer

    Nutritionix Link

    Copy the ingredients from below and adjust as needed.

    Copy Ingredients 
    Radishes - 500 GMS
Rock Salt - 1/2 TABLESPOON
Black Pepper - 1/2 TABLESPOON
Italian Herbs - 1/2 TABLESPOON
Olive Oil - 2 TABLESPOONS

    • Steps

      • Step 1: Wash and dry 500 gms radishes in a bowl.
      • Step 2: Add 1/2 Tablespoon Rock Salt, 1/2 Tablespoon Black Pepper.
      • Step 3: Add 1/2 Tablespoon (optional) Italian Herbs.
      • Step 4: Add 2 Tablespoons Olive Oil and mix well.
      • Step 5: Place in Air Fryer at 190C for about 20-25 minutes.
      • Step 6: Serve hot.
