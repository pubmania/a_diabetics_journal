    Keto French Fries

    Tastes great with Tomato Mint Pepper Spinach Dip, Tzatziki Dip and Basil Mint Pepper Garlic Dip.

    Info

    This can be prepared using Celeriac or Swede. I have found that while celeriac does not raise my BG, Swede at times does. There are people for whom both work just fine. I would suggest try both and see the impact. Personally, I do like Swede better but as it does spike my BG at times, I try to avoid it.

    Key Stats¶


    11.75 Net Carbs per Serving

    Prep Time: 10 minutes

    Cooking Time: 35 minutes

    Serves: 2 portions

    Type: Vegetarian

    Category: Continental

    • Ingredients¶

      S.No Ingredient Amount Step
      1. Celeriac 500 gms(1 Large) Step 1
      2. Extra Virgin Olive Oil 6 tablespoons Step 4
      3. Pink Himalayan Salt 1 teaspoon Step 4
      4. Black Pepper to taste
      5. Red Chilli Flakes to taste

    • Cookware¶


      1. Air Fryer
      2. Knife

    • Steps¶

      • Step 1: Cut 500 gms(1 Large) Celeriac into long fries sized pieces using knife.

      Note

      One can also use Swede instead of Celeriac, although Swede has slighly higher carbs.

      • Step 2: Wash and dry the cut pieces and place them in Air Fryer basket.
      • Step 3: Add 6 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil, 1 teaspoon Pink Himalayan Salt.
      • Step 4: Mix it well so the surface of Celeriac is all coated with the oil.
      • Step 5: Put it on air fry at 180 C for 25-30 minutes.
      • Step 6: Keep shaking the air fryer basket every 10 minutes so the celeriac is crisp on all sides.
      • Step 7: Once done, sprinkle black pepper (to taste), red chilli flakes (to taste).
      • Step 8: Serve hot.
