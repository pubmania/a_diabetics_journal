    Cheesy Bites

    Tastes great with Tomato Mint Pepper Spinach Dip, Green Pesto, Tzatziki Dip and Basil Mint Pepper Garlic Dip.

    Key Stats


    1.68 Net Carbs per Serving

    Cooking Time: 15 minutes

    Serves: 16

    Type: Vegetarian

    Category: Greek

White Wine Vinegar - 1 TSP
Baking Powder - 1/2 TSP

    • Steps

      • Step 1: Melt 1.5 cups grated mozzarella cheese and 2 tbsp cream cheese in a bowl using the microwave.
      • Step 2: Stir using a spatula until thoroughly combined.
      • Step 3: Add 1 cup almond flour, 1 tbsp (grated) Extra Mature Cheddar Cheese.
      • Step 4: Mix 1/2 tbsp flax seed with 1.5 tbsp (warm) water in a small bowl.
      • Step 5: Stir it vigorously using a fork and keep it aside for at least 5 minutes.
      • Step 6: After 5 minutes transfer flax seed and water mixture to the first bowl with rest of the ingredients.
      • Step 7: Add 1 tsp white wine vinegar and 1/2 tsp baking powder.
      • Step 8: Knead it into a smooth dough using hands.
      • Step 9: Divide the dough into 16 balls.
      • Step 10: Roll the balls in 2 tbsp (powdered) Extra Mature Cheddar Cheese.
      • Step 11: Preheat the air fryer to 200C.
      • Step 12: Once hot, place the balls in the air fryer basket and cook for 8 minutes at 200C.
      • Step 13: Serve Hot.
