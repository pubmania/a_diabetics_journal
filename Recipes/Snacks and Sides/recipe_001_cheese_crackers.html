    Cheese Crackers

    Key Stats


    2.8 Net Carbs per Serving

    Cooking Time: 30 minutes

    Serves: 6

    Type: Vegetarian

    Category: Savoury

    • Steps

      • Step 1: Take a mixer grinder jar and add 1 cup Almond Flour, 1 cup Grated Extra Mature Cheddar Cheese, Ground Black Pepper (to taste), Salt (to taste), Italian Herbs (to taste).
      • Step 2: Then pulse grind to mix well.
      • Step 3: Add 1 teaspoon water and pulse it in mixer again.
      • Step 4: Check for consistency and add 1 teaspoon water again
      • Step 5: Mix and pulse it again till the mixture is of a consistency to form a dough ball.
      • Step 6: Then take it out and place it on a parchment paper.
      • Step 7: Press it thin using rolling pin or tortilla press and place it on a plate.
      • Step 8: Using a knife or pizza cutter, create cuts for small squares - no need to separate.
      • Step 9: Microwave the flattened dough for about 1.5-2 minutes based on wattage, until golden brown.
      • Step 10: Take out from microwave and let it cool.
      • Step 11: Once cool and dry, break along the lines of cuts.
      • Step 12: Add extra seasoning (as needed) on top.
