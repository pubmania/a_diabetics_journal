    Paneer Crackers

    Key Stats


    2.39 Net Carbs per Serving

    Cooking Time: 30 minutes

    Serves: 6

    Type: Vegetarian

    Category: Savoury

    • Ingredients

      S.No Ingredient Amount Step
      1. Paneer 250 gms Step 1
      2. Ground Black Pepper to taste
      3. Red Chilli Flakes to taste
      4. Mint Powder to taste (optional)
      5. Salt to taste
      6. Almond Flour 100 gms Step 4
      7. Olive Oil 1 Tablespoon Step 5
      8. Extra Seasoning as needed

    Copy Ingredients 
    Paneer - 250 GMS
Ground Black Pepper - to taste
Red Chilli Flakes - to taste
Mint Powder - to taste (optional)
Salt - to taste
Almond Flour - 100 GMS
Olive Oil - 1 TABLESPOON
Extra Seasoning - as needed

    • Steps

      • Step 1: Chop 250 gms Paneer into small pieces using knife.
      • Step 2: Add it to a mixer grinder jar
      • Step 3: Then add Ground Black Pepper (to taste), Red Chilli Flakes (to taste), Mint Powder (to taste (optional)), Salt (to taste), Pulse and Grind till Paneer turns into dough.
      • Step 4: Take out the mixture and add 100 gms Almond Flour into the mix.
      • Step 5: Add 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil and mix it with hand till it starts shaping as a smooth dough ball.
      • Step 6: Mix till there is a consistency to form a dough ball.
      • Step 7: Then take it out and place it on a parchment paper.
      • Step 8: Press it thin using rolling pin or tortilla press and place it on a plate.
      • Step 9: Using a knife or pizza cutter, create cuts for small squares - no need to separate.
      • Step 10: Microwave the flattened dough for about 1.5-2 minutes based on wattage, until golden brown.
      • Step 11: Take out from microwave and let it cool.
      • Step 12: Once cool and dry, break along the lines of cuts.
      • Step 13: Add extra seasoning (as needed) on top.
