    Fried Courgette

    Key Stats¶


    5.45 Net Carbs per Serving

    Prep Time: 5 Minutes

    Cooking Time: 15 minutes

    Serves: 2

    Type: Vegetarian

    Category: Savoury

    • Ingredients¶

      S.No Ingredient Amount Step
      1. Courgette 400 gms(2 medium) Step 1
      2. Extra Virgin Olive Oil 2 tbsp Step 2
      3. Almond Flour 4 tbsp Step 3
      4. Salt to taste
      5. Freshly Ground Black Pepper to taste

    • Cookware¶


      1. Grater
      2. Spatula
      3. Air Fryer Basket

    Nutritionix Link

    Copy the ingredients from below and adjust as needed.

    Copy Ingredients 
    Courgette - 400 GMS
Extra Virgin Olive Oil - 2 TBSP
Almond Flour - 4 TBSP
Salt - to taste
Freshly Ground Black Pepper - to taste

    • Steps¶

      • Step 1: Wash 400 gms(2 medium) Courgette and using Grater grate into the Air Fryer Basket.
      • Step 2: Add 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil.
      • Step 3: Add 4 tbsp almond flour and mix well using spatula.
      • Step 4: Now air fry at 180C for about {12-15%minutes} while shaking in between.
      • Step 5: Once done, add salt (to taste), freshly ground black pepper (to taste) and mix well.
      • Step 6: Serve hot.
