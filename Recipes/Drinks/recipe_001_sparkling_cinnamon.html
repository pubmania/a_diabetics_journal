    Sparkling Cinnamon

    Tip

    The natural sweetness of Cinnamon makes it a terrific drink and great healthy replacement for all popular cold drinks and colas on market which are not good for anyone but especially disastrous for diabetics. Anyway, the drink goes really well with low carb pizza and works well at all other social occasions.

    Key Stats¶


    0.0 Net Carbs per Serving

    Cooking Time: 10 minutes

    Serves: 2 Litres

    Category: Drinks

    • Ingredients¶

      S.No Ingredient Amount Step
      1. Whole Cinnamon Sticks infuser full
      2. Water 0.5 L Step 2
      3. Sparkling Water 1.5 Litre Step 4
      4. Ice as needed

    • Cookware¶


      1. Teapot
      2. Kettle Or Microwaveable Jar
      3. Hob Or Microwave

    Nutritionix Link

    Copy Ingredients 
    Whole Cinnamon Sticks - infuser full
Water - 0.5 L
Sparkling Water - 1.5 LITRE
Ice - as needed

    • Steps¶

      • Step 1: Take Whole Cinnamon Sticks (infuser full) and put it in the teapot infuser.
      • Step 2: Boil 0.5 L water using a kettle or microwaveable jar on hob or microwave
      • Step 3: Once boiled, pour it into the teapot and let it steep overnight.
      • Step 4: Mix the steeped Cinnamon Tea with 1.5 Litre Sparkling water.
      • Step 5: Serve cold with ice (as needed)
