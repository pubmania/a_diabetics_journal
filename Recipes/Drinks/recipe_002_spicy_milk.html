    Spicy Milk (Masala Doodh)

    Key Stats


    14.11 Net Carbs per Serving

    Cooking Time: 10 minutes

    Serves: 2

    Category: Drinks

    • Ingredients

    Nutritionix Link

    Copy the ingredients from below and adjust as needed.

    Copy Ingredients 
    Cashew Nuts - 24 GMS
Almond Nuts - 18 GMS
Pistachio - 18 GMS
Whole Milk - 1 CUP
Double Thick Cream - 1 CUP
Green Cardamom Powder - 0.5 TSP
Nutmeg Powder - 0.5 TSP
Saffron - a pinch
Erythritol - 2 TBSP

    • Steps

      • Step 1: Add 24 gms (15) Cashew Nuts, 18 gms (15) Almond Nuts and 18 gms (15) Pistachio into a mixer jar.
      • Step 2: Grind them using mixer-grinder into fine powder.
      • Step 3: Now add 1 cup whole milk and 1 cup Double Thick cream into a saucepan.
      • Step 4: Put the saucepan on the hob at high heat and bring it to boil.
      • Step 5: Once the milk and cream has boiled, add the powdered nuts, 0.5 tsp Green Cardamom Powder, 0.5 tsp Nutmeg powder and Saffron (a pinch).
      • Step 6: Finally add 2 tbsp erythritol.
      • Step 7: Let it boil for 4-5 minutes and keep stirring with a spatula in between to make sure milk does not get burnt.
      • Step 8: The thickened drink can be served hot in winter or chilled with ice in summer.
