    Basil Mint Pepper Garlic Dip

    Tastes great with Tomato Keftedes, Keto French Fries, Protien Rich Cutlets and Cheesy Bites.

    Key Stats


    1.61 Net Carbs per Serving

    Prep Time: 5 minutes

    Cooking Time: 5 minutes

    Serves: 16 portions

    Type: Vegetarian

    Category: Dips

    • Ingredients

      S.No Ingredient Amount Step
      1. Fresh Mint Leaves 30 gms Step 2
      2. Fresh Basil Leaves 30 gms Step 3
      3. Green Chillies 25 gms(2-3 medium) Step 3
      4. Garlic 1 clove Step 3
      5. Orange Capsicum 150 gms(1 chopped) Step 4
      6. Red Onion 25 gms (1/4 chopped) Step 4
      7. Ginger 1 inch Step 4
      8. Virgin Olive Oil 1.5 tbsp Step 5
      9. Pink Himalayan Salt to taste
      10. Erythritol as needed

    • Cookware


      1. Mixer Grinder
      2. Smallest Mixer Jar

    • Steps

      • Step 1: Take smallest mixer jar.
      • Step 2: Add 30 gms fresh mint leaves.
      • Step 3: Add 30 gms fresh basil leaves, 25 gms(2-3 medium) green chillies and 1 clove garlic.
      • Step 4: Add 150 gms(1 chopped) orange capsicum, 25 gms (1/4 chopped) red onion and 1 inch ginger.
      • Step 5: Add 1.5 tbsp virgin olive oil.
      • Step 6: Finally add Pink Himalayan Salt (to taste).
      • Step 7: Grind using mixer grinder.
      • Step 8: If it's too spicy then add a little bit of erythritol (as needed).
      • Step 9: Serve it with snack dishes.
