    Tzatziki Dip

    Key Stats


    1.15 Net Carbs per Serving

    Cooking Time: 15 minutes

    Serves: 16

    Type: Vegetarian

    Category: Greek

    • Ingredients

      S.No Ingredient Amount Step
      1. Cucumber 240 gms Step 1
      2. Garlic 3 cloves Step 2
      3. Fresh Dill 1 tbsp Step 3
      4. Pink Himalayan Salt to taste
      5. Freshly Ground Black Pepper to taste
      6. Greek Yoghurt 1 cup Step 5
      7. Extra Virgin Olive Oil 1 tbsp Step 6

    • Cookware


      1. Grater
      2. Bowl
      3. Spatula
      4. Knife

    Nutritionix Link

    Copy the ingredients from below and adjust as needed.

    Copy Ingredients 
    Cucumber - 240 GMS
Garlic - 3 CLOVES
Fresh Dill - 1 TBSP
Pink Himalayan Salt - to taste
Freshly Ground Black Pepper - to taste
Greek Yoghurt - 1 CUP
Extra Virgin Olive Oil - 1 TBSP

    • Steps

      • Step 1: Using a grater coarsely grate 240 gms cucumber into a bowl.
      • Step 2: Now grate 3 cloves garlic into the bowl.
      • Step 3: Chop 1 tbsp fresh dill using knife and add to the bowl.
      • Step 4: Add Pink Himalayan Salt (to taste) and freshly ground black pepper (to taste).
      • Step 5: Add 1 cup Greek Yoghurt in the bowl and mix it well using spatula.
      • Step 6: Finally add drizzle 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil and mix lightly.
