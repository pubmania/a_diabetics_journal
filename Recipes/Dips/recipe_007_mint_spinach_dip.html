    Tomato Mint Pepper Spinach Dip

    Key Stats¶


    6.43 Net Carbs per Serving

    Prep Time: 5 minutes

    Cooking Time: 10 minutes

    Serves: 4 portions

    Type: Vegetarian

    Category: Indian Chutney

    • Steps¶

      • Step 1: Take smallest mixer jar.
      • Step 2: Add 50 gms(1/2 of mixer jar) fresh mint leaves.
      • Step 3: Add 50 gms(1/2 of mixer jar) fresh corriander leaves, 25 gms(2-3 medium) green chillies and 1 clove garlic.
      • Step 4: Add 15 gms(7-10 leaves) Baby Spinach and 1 tbsp Apple cider vinegar, 1 inch ginger.
      • Step 5: Add 250 gms(4 medium) vine tomatoes, 1.5 tbsp virgin olive oil, 25 gms(1/6 chopped) red Capsicum.
      • Step 6: Finally add Pink Himalayan Salt (to taste).
      • Step 7: Grind on mixer grinder.
      • Step 8: If it's too spicy then add a little bit of erythritol (as needed).
      • Step 9: Serve it with snack dishes.
