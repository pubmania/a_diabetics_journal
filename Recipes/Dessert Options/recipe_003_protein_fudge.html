    Pea Protein and Cashew Fudge

    • Steps¶

      • Step 1: Roast 60 gms Pea Protein in a small pan till it smells lightly roasted and not brown
      • Step 2: Put 125 gms unsalted butter, 120 ml double thick cream and 120 ml whole milk in a medium pan.
      • Step 3: Place the medium pan on medium to high heat and keep stirring with a spatula.
      • Step 4: When butter has melted and is bubbling a bit, add 80 gms erythritol, saffron (kesar) (Few strands).
      • Step 5: Then add 1 tsp(5 peeled crushed) cardamom and keep stirring.
      • Step 6: Keep stirring till the mixture starts to caremalise a bit (starts turning slightly brown)
      • Step 7: Once caremalised, add roasted pea protein, 1 tbsp dry dessicated coconut, 2 tbsp almond flour and 30 gms crushed cashew nuts.
      • Step 8: Add 5 gms unsalted butter and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract together.
      • Step 9: Keep stirring until it is all mixed and turns golden brown.
      • Step 10: Transfer to a baking tray to set and sprinkle 1 tsp(5 peeled crushed) cardamom on top.
      • Step 11: Takes about 30 to 40 minutes to set though it turns out good if left overnight.
