    No Egg Chocolate Cookies

    This recipe has a lovely story behind it.

    First-off it is not one of my experiements but that of my lovely daughter. She wanted to make something special for me on Father's Day and baked these without any help from me or her mum. These turned out to be very delicious indeed and needless to say made the father's day that extra bit special for me.

    Prep Time: 15 minutes

    Cooking Time: 30 minutes

    Serves: 16 cookies

    Type: Vegetarian

    Category: Dessert

    Copy Ingredients 
    Almond Flour - 500 GMS
Erythritol - 120 GMS
Cocoa Powder - 50 GMS
Bicarbonate Of Soda - 1/2 TSP
Coconut Cream - 150 ML
Whipping Cream - 150 ML
Salted Butter - 2 TBSP

    • Steps

      • Step 1: In a bowl, add 500 gms Almond Flour, 120 gms Erythritol, 50 gms Cocoa Powder and 1/2 tsp Bicarbonate of Soda.
      • Step 2: Then add 150 ml Coconut Cream, 150 ml Whipping Cream and 2 tbsp Salted Butter.
      • Step 3: Mix well till the mixture turns into a dough ball.
      • Step 4: Break the dough into small pieces to make 16 round cookie shapes.
      • Step 5: Place these on baking tray.
      • Step 6: Bake in the oven at 180C for about 20 minutes
