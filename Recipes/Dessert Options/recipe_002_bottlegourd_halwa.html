    Dessert Indian

    Bottlegourd (Lauki) Halwa

    Key Stats


    2.82 Net Carbs per Serving

    Cooking Time: 30 minutes

    Serves: 2 portions

    Type: Vegetarian

    Category: Dessert

    • Ingredients

      S.No Ingredient Amount Step
      1. Bottlegourd 1 Grated Step 1
      2. Clarified Butter (Ghee) 2 Tablespoons Step 2
      3. Double Cream 2 tablespoons Step 4
      4. Erythritol 2 tablespoons Step 5
      5. Cardamom Powder 1 teaspoon Step 7
      6. Cashew Nuts 8 gms(5 pieces) Step 8
      7. Almond Nuts 6 gms(5 pieces) Step 8
      8. Pistachio Nuts 6 gms(10 pieces) Step 8

    • Cookware


      1. Hob
      2. Pressure Cooker Or Steamer
      3. Pan

    Nutritionix Link

    Copy the ingredients from below and adjust as needed.

    Copy Ingredients 
    Bottlegourd - 1 GRATED
Clarified Butter (Ghee) - 2 TABLESPOONS
Double Cream - 2 TABLESPOONS
Erythritol - 2 TABLESPOONS
Cardamom Powder - 1 TEASPOON
Cashew Nuts - 8 GMS
Almond Nuts - 6 GMS
Pistachio Nuts - 6 GMS

    • Steps

      • Step 1: Place the 1 Grated bottlegourd into a pressure cooker or steamer and steam it for 3-5 minutes.
      • Step 2: Heat a pan and put 2 Tablespoons Clarified Butter (Ghee).
      • Step 3: Saute the steamed bottlegourd on medium heat until it turns slightly brown.
      • Step 4: Add 2 tablespoons double cream and cook it for another 5-7 minutes.
      • Step 5: Now add 2 tablespoons Erythritol and mix it well.
      • Step 6: Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
      • Step 7: Sprinkle 1 teaspoon cardamom powder and mix it well.
      • Step 8: Roast 8 gms(5 pieces) Cashew nuts, 6 gms(5 pieces) Almond nuts and 6 gms(10 pieces) Pistachio nuts
      • Step 9: Make a coarse powder of the roasted nuts.
      • Step 10: Turn off the hob and garnish with coarsely powdered mixture of roasted nuts.
      • Step 11: Serve hot.
