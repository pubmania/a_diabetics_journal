    Peanut Butter Mugcake

    A very quick one minute recipe from Joe Duff - YouTube Link.

    Info

    The measurement used in this recipe, results in a cake that I can't eat fully and for someone with diet similar to mine, I think it can easily serve two hence the portion size of 2.

    Key Stats¶


    4.46 Net Carbs per Serving

    Cooking Time: 3 minutes

    Serves: 2 portions

    Type: Vegetarian with Egg

    Source: External Link

    Category: Dessert

    • Ingredients¶

      S.No Ingredient Amount Step
      1. Baking Powder 1/2 tsp Step 1
      2. Erythritol 1 Tablespoon Step 1
      3. Egg 1 number Step 2
      4. Smooth Peanut Butter 3 Tablespoons Step 2
      5. Baking Spray as needed

    • Cookware¶


      1. Microwave
      2. Small Bowl
      3. Small Mug

    Nutritionix Link

    Copy the ingredients from below and adjust as needed.

    Copy Ingredients 
    Baking Powder - 1/2 TSP
Erythritol - 1 TABLESPOON
Egg - 1 NUMBER
Smooth Peanut Butter - 3 TABLESPOONS
Baking Spray - as needed

    • Steps¶

      • Step 1: Add 1/2 tsp Baking Powder and 1 Tablespoon Erythritol in a small bowl
      • Step 2: Add 1 number Egg and 3 Tablespoons Smooth Peanut Butter into the bowl.
      • Step 3: Mix until well combined and smooth
      • Step 4: Now coat a small mug with baking spray (as needed)
      • Step 5: Move the batter to the small mug
      • Step 6: Microwave for 30-60 seconds.
