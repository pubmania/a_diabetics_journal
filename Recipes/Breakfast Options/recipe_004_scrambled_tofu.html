    Scrambled Tofu (Tofu Bhurji)

    This goes well with Tava Roti as well as Wheat Gluten Bread.

    Key Stats


    13.99 Net Carbs per Serving

    Cooking Time: 20 minutes

    Serves: 2 portions

    Type: Vegetarian

    Category: Indian

    • Ingredients

      S.No Ingredient Amount Step
      1. Green Chillies 2-3 medium size Step 1
      2. Tomato 100 gms(1 Large) Step 1
      3. Ginger 1 tbsp(1 inch) Step 1
      4. Red Onion 200 gms(1 Large) Step 2
      5. Garlic 3 cloves Step 2
      6. Avocado Oil 3 tablespoons Step 4
      7. Cumin 1 teaspoon Step 5
      8. Turmeric 1 teaspoon Step 5
      9. Red Chilli Powder 1 teaspoon Step 5
      10. Tofu 250 gms Step 7
      11. Pink Himalayan Salt to taste
      12. Pepper to taste

    • Steps

      • Step 1: Finely chop 2-3 medium size Green chillies, 100 gms(1 Large) Tomato and 1 tbsp(1 inch) Ginger using knife
      • Step 2: Cut 200 gms(1 Large) Red Onion and 3 cloves Garlic using knife into small pieces.
      • Step 3: Place all cut and chopped veggies in a bowl
      • Step 4: Pour 3 tablespoons Avocado Oil in a frying pan and put it for heating.
      • Step 5: Add 1 teaspoon cumin, 1 teaspoon turmeric and 1 teaspoon red chilli powder and keep stirring with spatula.
      • Step 6: After 45 seconds or so add cut and chopped veggies from bowl and keep stirring.
      • Step 7: Use a fork to mash 250 gms Tofu.
      • Step 8: Add the coarsely mashed tofu and keep stirring.
      • Step 9: Let it cook for 5-8 minutes.
      • Step 10: Add Pink Himalayan Salt (to taste) and pepper (to taste).
      • Step 11: Serve hot.
