    Fried Gobhi (Cauliflower)

    For this recipe one can use fresh cauliflower or pre-cut cauliflower that is available in frozen section of nearly all supermarkets. I use the latter version as I am lazy.If like me you are using frozen cauliflower, its important that you don't thaw it or else it will become watery.

    Tastes great with Tava Roti, Tandoori Roti and Naan Bread and also goes well with No Egg Wheat Gluten Bread.

    • Steps¶

      • Step 1: If using fresh 500 gms cauliflower, cut into small florets using knife
      • Step 2: Put the frying pan on flame and add 4 tablespoons Avocado Oil
      • Step 3: Once heated, add 1 teaspoon corriander powder, 1 teaspoon garam masala and 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
      • Step 4: Mix well with Spatula
      • Step 5: Then add 1 teaspoon Pink Himalayan Salt, 1 teaspoon red chilli powder, 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
      • Step 6: To make it spicy you can add 15 gms(1-2 finely chopped, optional) green chillies
      • Step 7: Now add 75 gms(1 small chopped) onion and saute it till it turns slightly brown.
      • Step 8: Finally, add the cut cauliflower florets into the frying pan and 2 tablespoons water
      • Step 9: Cook on high heat for 5 minutes, keep stirring and mixing.
      • Step 10: Now cover the frying pan and leave it on slow heat for 10-15 minutes
      • Step 11: While cauliflower is cooking, prepare garnish by taking a small frying pan and add 1 tablespoon ghee
      • Step 12: Put it on high heat and add 2.5 teaspoons red chilli powder
      • Step 13: Give it 20 seconds before taking the small frying pan off from heat immediately.
      • Step 14: Garnish the cooked cauliflower with content from small frying pan and freshly chopped 25 gms(1 small bunch) corriander leaves
      • Step 15: Serve hot.
