    Fried Bhindi (Ladyfinger / Okra)

    For this recipe one can use fresh okra or chopped frozen okra that is available in some Asian stores. I use the latter version (500 gms pack) as I am lazy.If like me you are using frozen okra, its important that you don't thaw it or else it will become watery.

    Tastes great with Tava Roti, Tandoori Roti and Naan Bread and also goes well with No Egg Wheat Gluten Bread.

    • Steps¶

      • Step 1: If using fresh 500 gms okra, chop it using knife
      • Step 2: Put the frying pan on flame and add 4 tablespoons Avocado Oil
      • Step 3: Once heated, add 1 teaspoon corriander powder, 1 teaspoon garam masala and 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
      • Step 4: Mix well with Spatula
      • Step 5: Then add 1 teaspoon Pink Himalayan Salt, 1 teaspoon red chilli powder, 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
      • Step 6: To make it spicy you can add 20 gms(1-2 finely chopped, optional) green chillies
      • Step 7: Now add 75 gms(1 small chopped) onion and saute it till it turns slightly brown.
      • Step 8: Finally, add the chopped Okra into the frying pan
      • Step 9: Cook until okra looses water and becomes crispy.
      • Step 10: Serve hot.
