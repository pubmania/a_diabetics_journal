    Feta and Mushroom Bake

    Key Stats


    8.15 Net Carbs per Serving

    Prep Time: 5 minutes

    Cooking Time: 5 minutes

    Serves: 2

    Type: Snack

    Category: Vegetarian

    • Ingredients

      S.No Ingredient Amount Step
      1. Mushrooms 200 gms Step 1
      2. Feta Cheese 25 gms Step 2
      3. Salted Butter 20 gms Step 3
      4. Chilli Olive Oil to taste
      5. Garlic Olive Oil to taste
      6. Italian Herbs 3 tsp Step 4
      7. Garlic Granules 1 tsp Step 4
      8. Onion Granules 1 tsp Step 4
      9. Balsamic Vinegar to taste

    • Cookware


      1. Spoon
      2. Microwave-Safe Bowl

    Nutritionix Link

    Copy the ingredients from below and adjust as needed.

    Copy Ingredients 
    Mushrooms - 200 GMS
Feta Cheese - 25 GMS
Salted Butter - 20 GMS
Chilli Olive Oil - to taste
Garlic Olive Oil - to taste
Italian Herbs - 3 TSP
Garlic Granules - 1 TSP
Onion Granules - 1 TSP
Balsamic Vinegar - to taste

    • Steps

      • Step 1: Break 200 gms mushrooms into pieces by hand and place in a microwave-safe bowl.
      • Step 2: Crumble 25 gms feta cheese over the mushrooms.
      • Step 3: Add 20 gms salted butter and drizzle with chilli olive oil (to taste) and garlic olive oil (to taste).
      • Step 4: Sprinkle with 3 tsp italian herbs, 1 tsp garlic granules, and 1 tsp onion granules.
      • Step 5: Mix with spoon and cover the bowl.
      • Step 6: Microwave on high (900W) 4.5 minutes until cooked.
      • Step 7: Garnish with balsamic vinegar (to taste).
      • Step 8: Serve hot.
